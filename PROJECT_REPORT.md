## 1. MOTIVATION

### Background
Matasree Super Industries, established in 2008, is a leading spices and masalas manufacturer and distributor based in Dehradun, India. With over 16 years of experience in delivering authentic, premium quality spices, the company has built a strong reputation across Indian households and businesses. However, the organization was lacking a modern digital presence to reach customers beyond traditional distribution channels.

### Business Drivers
**2.1 Market Opportunity**
- E-commerce for spices and specialty foods is rapidly growing in India
- Increasing smartphone penetration and online shopping adoption
- Customer demand for convenient home delivery of premium quality spices
- Opportunity to expand beyond geographical limitations

**2.2 Competitive Advantage**
- Leverage 16+ years of brand trust and quality reputation
- Differentiate through authentic Indian cultural branding
- Build direct customer relationships
- Reduce dependency on traditional retail intermediaries

**2.3 Organizational Goals**
- Establish online sales channel to complement existing business
- Increase market reach and customer base
- Create data-driven insights through customer interactions
- Enhance brand visibility in the digital space

### Strategic Impact
The Matasree Store will serve as a digital gateway to the company's extensive product portfolio, enabling:
- Direct-to-consumer sales with improved margins
- Customer data collection for personalized marketing
- 24/7 availability for customer interactions
- Scalability to handle growing demand

- **Payment Processing:** Razorpay API for secure transactions

### 5.4 Module Interactions

**Frontend Modules:**
- **pages/**: Page-level components (Home, Products, Cart, etc.)
- **components/**: Reusable UI components with traditional styling
- **services/**: API client and external service integrations
- **hooks/**: Custom React hooks for data fetching and state management
- **store/**: Zustand state management for global app state
- **types/**: TypeScript interfaces and type definitions
- **assets/**: Images, icons, and static media

**Backend Modules:**
- **routes/**: HTTP endpoint definitions (auth, products, orders, etc.)
- **controllers/**: Request handling and business logic
- **models/**: MongoDB schema definitions using Mongoose
- **middleware/**: Authentication, error handling, file uploads
- **utils/**: Helper functions (JWT, password hashing, email, payments)
- **config/**: Database connection and environment configuration
