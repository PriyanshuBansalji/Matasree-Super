## 🎯 Company Profile

Matasree Super Industries has been a trusted name in the Indian spice industry since 2008. Based in Clement Town, Dehradun, the company is dedicated to providing authentic, premium quality spices and masalas to households and businesses across India.

### Core Values
✓ **Purity** - No additives, preservatives, or artificial colors  
✓ **Quality** - Lab-tested for safety and authenticity  
✓ **Tradition** - Time-honored processing methods

- Turmeric & Turmeric Powders
- Coriander Products

- Combo Packs
